Something About Renaissance

Renaissance is the Annual Techno-Cultural Youth Fest Of Jaipur Engineering College & Research Center, Jaipur held every year in the month of March.

It is a national level techno-cultural festival. It gives a stage to grandstand the exceptional abilities from varoius Kind, For example , music, dance, drammatizations etc. Over the Years, it has been bestewoed by the nearness of varoius renowned personalities enhancing its legecy.

Renaissance 2022

Renaissance 2022 was held on 17 May to 19 May at the JECRC Foundation campus, Jaipur. On the first day 17 May, the Renaissance event started with the inauguration ceremony. After that, all other events started according to their schedule. At 8:30 pm the performance of the JAAL Band started. On the second day at 8:30 pm, the performance of DJ Kashish started, and then on 19 may at 8:30 pm the performance of Music MG (Millind Gaba) started. This is how Ren 2022 ended.
